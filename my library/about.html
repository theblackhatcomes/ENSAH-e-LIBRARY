Welcome to ENSAH Library

At ENSAH e-Library, we believe that books have the power to transform lives. Our mission is to provide easy access to a vast collection of literature, fostering a love for reading and knowledge.

Founded in 2024, ENSAH Library aims to bring together readers from the school of applied sciences of Al Hoceima. Whether you are seeking inspiration, entertainment, or education, ENSAH library offers something for everyone.
